New game jam project "Raccon Zoom"!

Small Raccon* in a big city? Time to change that!

*No - this is not a typo ;)

Go through obstacles and watch out for any traps. Your Magnifying Glass will definitely come in handy for that! Use it to increase the size of the boxes! (Psst! Decreasing the size is also a feature, but we have decided not to tell that, since people seem to like big things either way). Scale your way to the victory!
